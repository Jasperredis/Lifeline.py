Lifeline.py is a retro-style endless game consisting of a player on a line, where they can only move left and right. They must get heals to prevent death while life is slowly ticking down and enemies block their path.
Proudly presented by jasperredis (https://jris.straw.page) in open source.
This website contains install/GitHub links and online information for Lifeline.py to fetch.
